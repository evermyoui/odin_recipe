Description

a popular Filipino dish from Pampanga, made from finely chopped, boiled, and grilled parts of a pig's head, such as ears, cheeks, and face, mixed with chicken liver, onions, chili peppers, and seasoned with calamansi or vinegar

Ingredients

  • Chopped Pork Parts
  • Chicken Liver
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Chilies
  • Calamansi

Steps

  1. Combined Pork Belly.Fill with water until meat is covered
  2. Remove meat fromn pot for 10 minutes
  3. Chop pork belly
  4. Mix chopped pork in a bowl
  5. Add chicken livers mash and saute until cooked through
  6. Add pork to saute pan and add mayonnaise and combine
